EZ Esports Broadcasting Suite

overview

Developed custom Twitch overlay graphics for live broadcasting of competitive play in VALORANT, League of Legends, and Tetris complete with OBS scene integration and controls.

outcomes

Elevated broadcast production quality on Twitch by designing a cohesive overlay system with dynamic team name integration, sponsor rotation, and real-time game elements. Built a modular OBS scene collection with 6+ broadcast states - lobby, champion select, in-game, and end screen - featuring coordinated transitions, hotkey-controlled elements, and Twitch chat integration controlled by webhooks. The overlay architecture supported 20+ unique teams with seamless customization, accommodating tournament brackets, regular season play, and special events without requiring redesign between matches.
