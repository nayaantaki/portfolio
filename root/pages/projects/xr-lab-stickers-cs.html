XR Lab Stickers

overview

Designed a set of 4 stickers for the XR Lab at Northeastern University to support student engagement and lab advertising during campus outreach events. The stickers were designed to be visually appealing and representative of the lab's focus on immersive technologies, with the goal of attracting students to learn more about the lab's resources and opportunities.

outcomes

Expanded the XR Lab's campus presence by introducing immersive technology resources to a broader student audience. Through consistent outreach and engagement, drove increased student interest in XR Lab services and grew weekly reservation activity.
