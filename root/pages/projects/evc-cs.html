Elements of Visual Composition

overview

A comprehensive exploration of 2D art and design principles through the creation of a final portfolio showcasing mastery of visual composition techniques.

takeaways

Developed a foundational understanding of 2D art and design principles through both study and studio practice, exploring concepts including balance, rhythm, pattern, emphasis, contrast, unity, movement, and the core visual elements of line, shape, space, value, color, and texture. Coursework combined critical analysis of visual design theory with hands-on compositional and color exercises. Culminated in a final portfolio of original works demonstrating mastery of visual composition techniques and principles, showcasing the ability to create engaging and effective designs across a range of media and formats.
